Household Income Prediction of ACS 2022

This project applies machine learning to predict household income levels using socioeconomic, housing, and employment-related variables. The dataset originates from the American Community Survey (ACS) 2022 Public Use Microdata Sample (PUMS) and includes 819,000+ records from 26 U.S. states. Also, itdemonstrates the effectiveness of ensemble ML models in analyzing and predicting socioeconomic patterns from large-scale public data. Insights can inform public policy, housing affordability studies, and economic development programs.
